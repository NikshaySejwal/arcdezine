Meet Our Founder

Ar. Nishant Singh

Founder & Principal Architect

Hello, I'm Ar. Nishant Singh, and I'm on a mission to continually expand my expertise in architecture, planning, and interior design at a professional level. With a firm belief in dedication, hard work, and a relentless eagerness to learn, I bring value to any team I'm a part of.

My academic journey commenced at the Manav Rachna International Institute of Research & Studies, where I pursued a Bachelor's degree in Architecture, before delving into interior design and then returning to architecture. This diverse educational background has provided me with a comprehensive understanding of spatial design principles from various perspectives.

Beyond my professional interests, I'm deeply passionate about cricket, sketching, web series, PC games, and music, all of which serve as wellsprings of creativity and inspiration.
